## Scope

TPS defines the **design, interaction, data, and behavior standards** for professional trading platform interfaces, covering:

- 📐 UI Foundations — Color, Typography, Spacing, Motion, Accessibility
- 🧩 Components — Quote, Candlestick, Order Entry, Watchlist, Order Book, etc.
- 📊 Business Domains — Order, Account, Execution, Settlement, Margin, Options, Futures
- 🌍 Markets — US, HK, CN, JP, SG, EU

---

## Principles

1. **AI-Native First** — Every spec is machine-readable and AI-consumable
2. **Precision** — Trading data requires exact formatting, no ambiguity
3. **Consistency** — One standard across platforms, markets, and frameworks
4. **Openness** — Community-driven, Apache 2.0 licensed
